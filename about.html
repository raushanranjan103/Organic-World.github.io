About Us

We are bunch of handful people who are deeply in love with Nature, are passionate about living a Sustainable Lifestyle and helping others to do same. As and when we started to realize the imortance of Sustainable livelihood & its positive impact on our Nature, we made an effort to shift from convenient-harmful-anti-nature lifestyle to eco-friendly-Zero-Waste lifestyle.

In the beginning it was very difficult to find and get our hands on alternative eco-friendly products in the market. This made our Journey to #maketheswitch more difficult and troublesome. There had to be many people like us who dearly wanted to Make the Switch, but unavailability of easy eco-friendly alternatives was a disappointment and hurdle in their decision. This rocky journey gave birth to the idea of Organic World Store.

Our Mission

Delivering best quality locally grown Organic produce. Providing products in their utmost natural form, as nature intended it to be. No Premium pricing.To Create a Zerowaste. To make available Sustainable and Eco-friendly products easily. No Premium pricing.

Being part of Zero Waste movement and revolution. To reduce single use plastic consumption at consumer level. To reduce thrash!#Refuse#Reduce#Reuse#Recycle. To make exceptional range of products available to our Zero Waste lovers. We believe in standing WITH our local Farmers & Artists and supporting them.
